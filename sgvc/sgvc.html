  San Gabriel Valley Consortium

The mission of the San Gabriel Valley Consortium on Homelessness is to facilitate partnerships, educate the community and member agencies, and advocate for appropriate housing and services.   -source

SGVC has developed a Google web map that displays homeless services and providers. In its current state, the map is limited in functionality and content.

A new responsive web map built in  ArcGIS will allow SGVC to meet its mapping goals.

  Web Map Proposal

The San Gabriel Valley Consortium can link ArcGis   to the current landing page.

The web map will have the following data as map layers.

  • Community Development Commission public housing and project areas
  • County tax assessor parcels
  • Department of Children and Family Service Areas
  • Department of Public Social Services public offices
  • Fire stations
  • Homeless populations in schools
  • Homelessness counts and cluster locations
  • Hospitals
  • Housing type
  • Libraries
  • Mental health providers
  • Police stations
  • Schools
  • Zoning/planning/urban renewal zones and areas

the user can toggle these layers on and off.
