San Gabriel Valley Consortium

  Proposal

The San Gabriel Valley Consortium can have a web page that links from the current landing page.

This web page would have a series of maps that are divided into subjects:

  • Clothing
  • Education
  • Food
  • Health
  • Job Training
  • Shelter
  • Legal Services

The maps themselves will have specific key terms.

For example: Health Map will have information about: Mental Health, substance abuse, and general medical services.

  Technology

The web page will be built with standard HTML/CSS/Javascript, and the Vue JavaScript framework

the maps will be made with BatchGeo and ArcGis.
